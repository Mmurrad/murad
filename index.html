Saydul Akbar Murad

About

Saydul Akbar Murad is a dedicated researcher currently pursuing his PhD in Computer Science at the University of Southern Mississippi, USA. He has previously completed his master's degree in Malaysia and his undergraduate education in Bangladesh. He has experience as a lecturer at the International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh. His current research focus is on Brain-Computer Interface (BCI), building upon his past work in P2P Communication and cloud computing. Additionally, Murad serves as an editor for the Journal of Computer Science, contributing his deep knowledge and expertise in the field.
